RISK LEVEL PREDICTOR FOR SUSPICIOUS FIRMS BEFORE AUDIT

Denotations :-
Sector Score : Historical risk score value of the target-unit .
Parameter A : Discrepancy found in the planned expenditure of inspection and summary report in Rs (crore).
Parameter B : Discrepancy found in the unplanned expenditure of inspection and summary report in Rs (crore).
Total : Total amount of discrepancy found in other reports in Rs (crore).
Money Value : Amount of money involved in misstatements in the past audits.
Note:-
A risk score below 2.5 means the company is not suspicious
A risk score between 2.5 and 4 means the company is moderately suspicious
A risk score above 4 means the company is very suspicious
